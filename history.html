History

Our company, founded by Candelario Lopez Robles, has a long and rich history dating back to the 1980s, when Candelario, a Mexican immigrant, arrived in California. He began his career in the gardening field, but through hard work and determination, he eventually started his own landscaping and design firm. Over the years, our company has grown and flourished, earning a reputation as an award-winning design and construction firm. Candelario has worked with notable figures such as the mayor of Lancaster, Rex Parris, and has collaborated with Daniel Jacobs, the CEO of the Antelope Valley fairgrounds, to rejuvenate the space and transform it into a world-class events center. In addition, Candelario co-founded Amigos De La Feria, an institution that aims to bring a Hispanic business presence to the Antelope Valley. Candelario is known for his dedication and expertise, and has built a strong reputation in the community. We are proud of our roots and the hard work and dedication that has brought us to where we are today. As we look to the future, we remain committed to providing the highest quality services to our clients and to upholding the values of hard work and excellence that have defined our company from the very beginning.

Cande Begins to Work Under His Mentor David Byerly.

1988

Cande began working under the mentorship of David Byerly, an experienced and respected figure in the industry. Working under David allowed Cande to gain valuable hands-on experience and learn from someone with a wealth of knowledge and expertise. He took every opportunity to learn from David and absorb as much as he could, knowing that this would be key to his success in the future. Cande's dedication and hard work paid off, and he eventually started his own successful landscaping and design firm, building on the foundation of knowledge and skills that he gained while working under David's mentorship.

Cande Founded His Gardening Company Providing Lawn and Sprinkler Service.

1994

Cande founded his gardening company in 1994 with the goal of providing top-notch lawn and sprinkler services to his customers. He had a passion for gardening and a strong desire to help people transform their outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional areas. With years of experience in the industry, Cande was well-equipped to handle a variety of tasks, from mowing and trimming to installing and maintaining sprinkler systems. Over the years, his company grew and gained a reputation for excellent service and attention to detail. Today, Cande's gardening company continues to thrive, thanks to his dedication to his craft and his commitment to providing top-notch service to every one of his customers.

Cande Acquires Landscape Contracting License and Expands Services and Expertise

2002

Cande received his landscape contracting license, which opened up new doors for his gardening company. With this added expertise, he was able to take on more complex and ambitious projects, including both residential and commercial work. Cande was thrilled to have the opportunity to create beautiful outdoor spaces for his clients, and he poured his heart and soul into each project. His attention to detail and dedication to quality helped him stand out in the industry, and he quickly gained a reputation as a top-notch landscape contractor. Whether working on a small residential project or a large commercial development, Cande always approached each job with enthusiasm and a focus on delivering the best possible results.

Cande Expands Offerings and Expertise with Swimming Pool Contractors License.

2005

Cande made a significant expansion to his professional offerings and expertise by obtaining his swimming pool contractors license. This achievement allowed Cande to provide a wider range of services to his clients, including the design and construction of swimming pools and related outdoor features. With this new license, Cande was able to demonstrate his expertise in the field and enhance his reputation as a reliable and skilled contractor. As a result of this expansion, Cande was able to take on more complex projects and meet the diverse needs of his clients.

Cande Finds AMEX Rocks, a Building Material Store Specializing in All Aspects of Construction in the Antelope Valley, in 2007

2007

Cande took a significant step in his career by founding AMEX Rocks, a building material store located in the Antelope Valley. Specializing in providing materials for all aspects of construction, including framing, roofing, and masonry, AMEX Rocks quickly became a go-to destination for contractors and DIY enthusiasts in the area. Cande's extensive knowledge and experience in the construction industry allowed him to curate a selection of high-quality products and assemble a team of expert staff to assist customers with product selection and installation. With his vision and leadership, AMEX Rocks became a successful and respected business in the community, and Cande was proud to have created a reliable source of building materials for the region.

Cande Lopez Contributes to the Initial Rejuvenation Project at the AV Fairgrounds

2009

Cande Lopez played a key role in bringing the initial rejuvenation project at the AV Fairgrounds to life. As a contractor with expertise in construction and landscaping, Cande was called upon to contribute his skills and knowledge to this important project. Working alongside a team of professionals, Cande helped to revitalize various areas of the fairgrounds, including the addition of new structures, the renovation of existing facilities, and the beautification of outdoor spaces. Through his hard work and dedication, Cande helped to enhance the overall appeal and functionality of the fairgrounds, making it a more enjoyable and welcoming place for the community to gather. The success of this project was a testament to Cande's expertise and commitment to quality, and he was proud to have played a part in its completion.

Cande Retires AMEX Franchise and Focuses on Design and Construction of Commercial and Residential Outdoor Oasis

2013

Cande made the decision to retire his AMEX franchise and focus on perfecting his design craft. With years of experience in the construction and landscaping industries, Cande had developed a strong reputation for creating beautiful and functional outdoor spaces. He decided to shift his focus to design and construction of commercial and residential outdoor oasis, using his expertise to create unique and inviting environments for clients. This transition allowed Cande to fully utilize his skills and creativity, and he was able to take on more complex and challenging projects. As a result, Cande's business continued to thrive and he was able to provide exceptional service to his clients.

Cande Lopez Co-Founds Amigos de la Feria Association in the Antelope Valley

2018

Cande Lopez co-founded the Amigos de la Feria Association in the Antelope Valley, a Hispanic association dedicated to bringing Hispanic celebrations, education, and business opportunities to the community. With a passion for cultural diversity and a desire to make a positive impact in the region, Cande joined forces with other community leaders to establish this organization. The Amigos de la Feria Association quickly gained popularity and support, and it became a valuable resource for the Hispanic community in the Antelope Valley. Through a variety of events, programs, and initiatives, the association promoted the celebration of Hispanic culture and provided education and support for Hispanic businesses in the area.
