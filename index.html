ABOUT US

Welcome to Cande and Associates, a leading design firm specializing in outdoor living. Based in Southern California, our elite team of engineers, architects, and designers have delivered hundreds of projects throughout the Golden State, ranging from large outdoor commercial centers to residential outdoor havens. At Cande and Associates, we are passionate about creating beautiful, functional outdoor spaces that exceed our clients' expectations. Whether you are looking to transform your backyard into a luxurious oasis or design an inviting outdoor area for your business, we have the expertise and creativity to bring your vision to life. Our team works closely with each client to understand their needs and preferences, and we take pride in delivering high-quality, personalized service. From initial concept to final construction, we are with you every step of the way, ensuring that the final result is a space that you will love for years to come. Thank you for considering Cande and Associates for your outdoor living needs. We look forward to working with you and helping bring your dream space to life.
